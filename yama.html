Yamarita Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large yams
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups vegetable oil (for frying)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the yams into thick strips.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, garlic powder, paprika, onion powder, thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and add water to create an egg wash.
  4. Dip each yam strip into the egg wash and then coat with the seasoned flour mixture.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a pan for frying.
  6. Fry the coated yam strips until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from the oil and place on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Yamarita!
