Recipe For Creamy Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces (about 225g) fettuccine or your preferred pasta
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: Garlic (minced), nutmeg, parsley for additional flavor

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta

    Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce

    In a large skillet or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. If you're using garlic, saut√© it in the butter until fragrant.

  3. Add Cream

    Pour in the heavy cream, stirring continuously. Allow the mixture to heat but avoid boiling.

  4. Add Cheese

    Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously to melt it into the sauce. If desired, you can also add a pinch of nutmeg for additional flavor.

  5. Season

    Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste. Remember that Parmesan is salty, so be cautious with the salt.

  6. Combine with Pasta

    Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing it to coat evenly.

  7. Serve

    Once the pasta is well-coated in the creamy sauce, serve it immediately. Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan if desired.
