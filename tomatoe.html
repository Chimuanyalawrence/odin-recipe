Tomato Omelet Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they are translucent.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and bell pepper to the pan and cook until they soften.
  5. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables in the pan.
  6. As the eggs start to set, gently lift the edges with a spatula and tilt the pan to let the uncooked eggs flow to the edges.
  7. If using cheese, sprinkle it over one half of the omelet.
  8. Once the eggs are fully set but still moist, fold the omelet in half with the spatula.
  9. Slide the omelet onto a plate and serve hot.
